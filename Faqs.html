DEFINITIONS & TERMS

WHAT ARE SENSES?

Every person has senses that help them understand and respond to environmental stimuli. Each sense sends information to the brain to help us understand and perceive the world around us. Any person can struggle and have difficulties with their senses, receiving and responding to information. All our products are designed to cater to a variety of needs — suitable for all, including kids with autism, ADHD or OCD.
