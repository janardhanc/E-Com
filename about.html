    We all know the importance of early intervention in the developmental learning of kids with sensory issues. Sensory House was born in early 2020 to make Multi-Sensory Rooms and associated products, accessible to the masses at an affordable price without compromising on quality!

    From first-hand knowledge and experience, the team at Sensory House are fed up with manufacturers taking advantage of parents, teachers and OT’s by what we feel are unreasonable mark ups.

    "Our aim is to make sensory mainstream through our core values of affordable pricing, superior quality, great after service and a simple ‘plug & play’ installation. This model enables us to offer an incredible 59 different items, for €6,995 while flying under the Made in Ireland banner and supporting those with sensory difficulties."
    – Keith O’Grady, MD

    As we grow we hope to manufacture everything in house, but as we are only a new venture finding our feet, we want to first prioritise getting a Sensory Room into every home, school, hospital and corporate body that needs it. We want to give every person access to sensory facilities and know that affordable prices will facilitate this.

    We are disrupting the Sensory Market for the better.
